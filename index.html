Introduction

Accounting is the recording and analysis of the accuracy in financial transactions involving the flow of money in and out of a business. Accountants provide solutions for businesses and individuals after examining financial records, identifying and evaluating potential areas of opportunity and risk. With advancing technology, things have become easier for accountants, but there is still room for improvements.

Xero: Best for Payments
  • Affordable price
  • Better for android than iOS
  • Invoicing
  • Unlimited users

Intuit QuickBooks Online: Best for Small Businesses
  • Mobile app
  • Send invoices with attached receipts with an encrypted link
  • Automatically track expenses

  • High starting price
  • Poor customer service

FreshBooks Accounting Software: Best for Invoicing
  • Customizable recurring invoices that sync with time tracking features
  • Good for apple/google users
  • Automatic late payment reminders for customers
  • High price-per-user fee
  • Fewer features with app than desktop

Zoho Books Accounting Software: Best for Micro Businesses
  • Builds automated workflows
  • Plans with inventory management
  • No payroll processing
  • Limited integration with other apps (non-Zoho)
  • Integration with free accounting tools (Zoho invoice)

Sage: Best for Basic Features
  • Low starting price
  • Online financial education courses
  • Thorough mobile invoice
  • Limited features with cheapest plan
  • Only a handful of iOS users

Potential Future of Accounting

PAID (Portable Accounting Integrated Device)
  • Use of AI - categorize information
  • Can directly print a document onto the computer
  • Scan document
  • Autocomplete the records when documents are complete
  • Allow user to edit on the go
  • Wireless connectivity
  • Multiple scans per minute
  • Low cost to buy
  • Low cost to make
  • Highly efficient

FEAA (Fast Efficient Accounting App)
  • Low cost to pay for use
  • Low cost to make
  • High efficiency
  • Unlimited files
  • Save files/edits automatically
  • Allow user to undo any edit(s)
  • Compare documents and show errors
